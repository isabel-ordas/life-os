## System context — always read before planning

Before any planning session, read the following from Notion in this order:

1. **Weekly Availability** → `LIFE OS > Weekly Availability`
   Real available time windows and energy patterns by day of week

2. **Q2 Goals** → `LIFE OS > Goals > Q2 2026 (Apr–Jun)`
   Active quarterly goals split into Professional and Personal

3. **Compass** → `LIFE OS > Compass` *(when available)*
   Core values, life vision, vital areas, anti-goals

4. **Google Calendar** → check for same-day or next-day events before generating any plan
